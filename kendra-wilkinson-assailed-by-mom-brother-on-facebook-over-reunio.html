Kendra Wilkinson Assailed By Mom and Brother on Facebook Over Reunion With Dad

July 24, 2024
blog

Kendra Wilkinson’s brother Colin and mom Patti are venting their frustrations on Facebook over her suddenly renewed bond with her long estranged father.

Both imply that Eric Wilkinson, who we saw reunite with his daughter on Kendra on Top Season 3 Episode 12 this weekend, if after Kendra’s money.

Which poses a few questions / points …

  • How much money does Kendra Wilkinson actually have?
  • Eric seems to be doing pretty darn well in Costa Rica.
  • What is Patti Wilkinson’s motivation in all of this?

    • It makes you wonder. That’s all we’re saying.

    In any case, apparently the events of this summer didn’t sit well with Patti and Eric Wilkinson, who writes, “Who would consider a man that left them with nothing.”

    “Never attempted to be involved in your life and never helped at all.”

     

    Patti went off on Facebook right after the Costa Rica reunion, as seen on Kendra on Top Season 3 Episode 13, and it looks like tensions are still high.

    Colin sneered about Kendra’s trip to visit Eric with her family, “He may excepted [sic] her request out of a thought of getting money. Making his name bigger.”

    Patt, meanwhile, ranted on her page, “Omg… Kendra never gave us any $$$$$$. She paid for Colin & I to go on a couple trips 10 yrs ago and that’s it!!!!!!”

    Colin also addressed his followers with this dig: “Ask us how much me and my mother and my grandma have made off my sis. Not a damn dime.”

    It seemed for a time that Patti would be a shoulder to cry on for Kendra in the wake of her husband Hank Baskett cheating on her with Ava London.

    Not so fast, it turns out.

    Patti was livid when Hank went to Costa Rica with Kendra to see Eric and went off on Facebook, writing, “I continue to be hurt and disrespected by my daughter.”

    Is she really in a position to talk, or complain? Regardless, Patti and Colin continue to make their feelings known, and public, as far as Kendra is concerned.

    As for K-Dubs, she has yet to respond to any of this, although she did shoot down rumors (sparked by her own quotes) that Wilkinson is single after dumping Hank.
