Who is Sweaty Betty owned by?

The activewear brand is British, founded by Tamara and Simon Hill-Norton in 1998.

It began with one boutique in Notting Hill and has since expanded to numerous shops in the UK and worldwide, plus its online store.

What are Sweaty Betty leggings made of?

Sweaty Betty leggings have been carefully curated to suit all different kinds of exercise and movement, making sure you're stylish comfortable for every workout.

Different legging styles have different purposes, for example the bestselling Power range is perfect for workouts and studio sessions. This means the fabric is super comfortable and stretchy, as well as being squat proof for a range of activities.

For more intense sessions and a range of sports, the Multi-Sport core range boasts a seamless look with moisture -wicking, athletic fabric to keep you cool and comfortable during your hard work.

How ethical is Sweaty Betty?

Sweaty Betty takes sustainability seriously: 25% of its products are made from either natural or recycled fabrics, using sustainable processes, in order to be more eco-friendly.

Plus it aims to remove all single use plastic from the business by 2025.

Sweaty Betty also has a foundation dedicated to supporting teenage girls from lower-income families and ethnically diverse communities. 

Is Sweaty Betty clothing worth it?

Yes, Sweaty Betty clothing doesn't come cheap. You can find alternatives from other brands, like workout gear and loungewear, at lower prices.

But it's generally agreed that Sweaty Betty clothing is much better quality, meaning it will last longer and support you much better - whether you're exercising or need a warm coat for winter.

On the whole, it's worth the investment.
