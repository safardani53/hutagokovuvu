But who is the former prison inmate turned self-professed lifestyle guru and what is his net worth? Here's everything you need to know.

Who is Wes Watson?

Wes Watson was born on November 19, 1983, in California.

He is an actor, life coach, motivational speaker and fitness coach from San Diego.

His experience of prison in his youth inspired him to start his own podcast, titled GP Penitentiary Life, where he discusses his philosophy and how he turned his life around.

Since his release, he has started his own personal training firm Watson Fit.
