Biography

David Beckham was born on May 2, 1975, in Leytonstone, London, England. His father, Ted Beckham, and mother, Sandra Georgina, gave birth to him. Ted was a kitchen fitter, while Sandra was a hairdresser. His two sisters, Joanne Louise Beckham, and Lynne Georgina Beckham raised him. He was born under the sign of Taurus.

Childhood

David has always wanted to play football since he was a child. He spent a lot of time playing in Ridgeway Park as a kid. He began his education at Chase Lane Primary School. David then enrolled at Chingford County High School. He had aspired to be a footballer since he was in elementary school.

David’s parents were Manchester United fans, and it was through them that the club influenced David. During his studies, he was given the opportunity to work as a trainee with Barcelona. He eventually joined the Tottenham Hotspur football club. He was a member of the Brimsdown Rovers team at the time. Then, in 2013, he signed a five-month contract with French club Paris Saint-German. David announced his retirement from professional football after the season ended.

How Did David Beckham Pursue His Career?

David Beckham’s life changed forever in 1991 when he accepted the Manchester United Youth Training Scheme. He was a youth player at the time, and he helped the club win the September FA Youth Cup. His ability earned him a spot on the senior team. He eventually signed a contract with Manchester United in January 1993.

He spent a season at Preston North End before returning to Manchester United. In 1995, David played as a right-sided attacking midfielder. The following year, he led the team to a Premier League and FA Cup double. Furthermore, he began his international career in England only in 1996. He played in the World Cup in France against Columbia and scored a goal.

Who is David Beckham Wife?
