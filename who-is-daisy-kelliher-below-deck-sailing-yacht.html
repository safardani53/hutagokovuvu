Who is Below Deck Sailing Yacht star, Daisy Kelliher?

Daisy Kelliher was born in Ireland in 1988.

The sailor is part of Captain Glenn Shephard's crew aboard the Parsifal III, serving as a chief stewardess.

She is not the first in her large family to set sail.

According to her Bravo biography, Kelliher's grandfather represented Ireland as a sailor in the 1964 Olympics. Her parents also worked on yachts, along with her aunts and uncles.
