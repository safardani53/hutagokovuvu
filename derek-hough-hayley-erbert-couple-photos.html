Derek Hough and Hailey Erbert are married! See photos of the dancing couple here.

Derek Hough and Hayley Erbert are the cutest couple ever! Despite their ten year age difference, their relatoinship is totally solid, and has been since they first got together. The exact time of the beginning of this relationship is not clear, but it’s speculated that they started dating in 2015. However, the two actually met before they got together romantically, Hayley was a dancer on Derek and Julianne Hough’s MOVE Live on Tour in 2014. She then became a troupe member on season 21 of ‘Dancing with the Stars,’ while Derek was competing on the show with Bindi Irwin. After Derek left ‘DWTS’ in 2016, he and Hayley stayed together, and have been a happy couple ever since! In June 2022, the two got engaged after seven years together and the couple got married on August 24, 2023.
