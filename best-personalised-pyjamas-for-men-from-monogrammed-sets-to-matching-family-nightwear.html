Best Personalised Pyjamas For Men 2019 | The Sun UK

April 16, 2024

SLEEPWEAR we wear for up to eight hours a day, which is why we should take pride in the clothes we put on for bed.

While some may like to suit up in a onesie to catch their 40 winks, others may prefer to wear a pair of shorts or even match with their loved one's bedtime attire.

So what are the best personalised pyjamas for men?

Monogrammed loungewear has been a huge hit with celebrity faces, including Dan Osborne and AaronMcLeod, as it is perfect to wear around the home, outside, and for bed.

Personalised pieces are all the more fun when you match with your partner, and family - especially over Christmas.

The customised detail can include embroidered initials or longer names, as well as PJs that are personal to suit your sleep needs.

1. Best celebrity loved personalised pyjama set

Forever Sewing are a celebrity-loved brand, as their pyjama sets and loungewear designs have been worn by TOWIE's Dan Osborne, as well as Ex On The Beach star Aaron McLeod, and many more.

The brand pride themselves in catering for every customers needs as each item is handmade and embroidered to your desire, whether it is for a short sleeve and short combo, longer length loungewear pieces, tracksuit sets, or accessories - you name it they can make it.

Men can choose from leggings to tracksuit, in both a slim fit or loose fit, as well as shorts, a matching t-shirt, or jumper, in cotton or a knitted design, all of which are available for women and children so the whole family will not miss out.

  • Matching Set for £13 to £30 from Forever Sewing - buy here 

2.  Best personalised pyjama sets for the family

Mini Lunn have created a selection of pyjamas, all of which feature collar detail and piping around the hem, for families to wear.

Father and son can co-ordinate in matching navy blue sets with white piping, while the mother and daughter versions come in white with red hem detail.

The best part of the His and Hers set is there is no word limit on the personalised text.

  • Mini Lunn His And Hers Personalised Cotton Pyjamas For £39.50 each from Not On The High Street - buy here 

3.  Best pyjama set for personalised comfort

Dagsmejan pride themselves on making sure every customer has a restful night's sleep because of their unique Swedish designs and Swiss textile engineering - did we mention they are a sustainable brand too?

The Balance Pyjama collection are made from innovative sleep technology, which works to moderate your temperature as it will keep you warm without overheating or sweating and cool you down.

The material is soft to the touch, lightweight, absorbent and is six times more breathable than cotton, while the seams are purposefully moved to the front of the body to ensure nothing irritates the skin, which could disrupt anyone's sleep pattern.

  • Balance Set Pyjamas £73 for each garment from Dagsmejan - buy here 

4.  Best personalised pyjama sets for Christmas

With Christmas fast approaching there is no better treat for a family than a set of matching festive pyjamas.

Bobora's adorable set comes with tartan printed bottoms, as well as green slogan long sleeve tops, with the names of the parents emblazoned on the front of them, as well as a children's version so the kids do not miss out.

  • (AD) BOBORA Christmas Pyjamas Family Cotton Sleepwear Set ranging from £5.09 from Amazon - buy here 

5.  Best monogramming pyjama sets for men

Monogramming an item of clothing can sometimes prove costly with fees being added on per letter at some brands, but not My 1st years.

This trendy men's pyjama set comes complete with a checked pyjama bottom, and pale blue long sleeve top to match, although it is only the top that can be embroidered.

The customisation is completely free, although there is a limit of up to nine words.

  • Personalised Blue Checked Adult Pyjamas for £45 from My 1st Years - buy here

6.  Best personalised onesies

Onesies are always a popular buy when winter hits, because they guarantee ultimate comfort and warmth on those cold nights.

Edward Sinclair's hooded onesie is the ideal purchase for those who are looking for an alternative option to a pyjama set.

This design comes in a selection of colours, sizes to suit three year old children up to XXL sizes for men, all of which can be personalised with an initial.
