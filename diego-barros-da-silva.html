Who is Diego Barros Da Silva:

Diego Barros Da Silva is a famous World Music Singer. He was born on June 19, 1986 and his birthplace is Cuiabá, Brazil. Diego is also well known as, Singer best known as one half of the Brazilian sertaneja duo Henrique & Diego. He and bandmate Luiz Henrique Teixeira have scored major hits with such tracks as “Suite 14,” “Esqueci Você” and “Raspao,” all of which topped the Brazilian Hot 100 chart. 

Diego is originated from Brazil. His song “Festa Boa” features Gusttavo Lima.
