After a week of anticipation, FBI: International is back with a new episode. This episode follows an exciting episode about an American business mogul involved in a murder in Frankfurt, Germany. The next episode, Snakes, will be released on March 8, 2022.

FBI: International- A COVID-19 sensitive case?

Despite lacking a promotional trailer, FBI: International has released some set photographs that offer a good idea of what to expect in the upcoming episode. It is not as apparent as a trailer, but it reveals specific details such as the location and the cast. According to the CBS synopsis for the forthcoming episode,

“A couple escapes to Albania while awaiting sentencing for defrauding the US government of millions in COVID-19 relief funding; Kellett works the case from Budapest as she and Forrester strive to reclaim their professional limits.”

As it relates to COVID-19, it appears this case will delve into specific social and political implications. Given the current scenario, this will be a complicated subject. Fans of the show should expect a dramatic episode that will test the team’s resolve.
