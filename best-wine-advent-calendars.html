Where to buy wine advent calendars in the UK?

Getting an advent calendar that's the leadup to Christmas can feel slow, so it's definitely important to get an advent calendar that's filled with wines you're going to enjoy.

How much do wine advent calendars cost?

Wine advent calendars are a lot more exciting than the standard chocolate ones we've come to know and love, but the biggest difference is the price.

If you're looking for a boozy box on the leadup to Christmas, you'll be looking at spending around £70 for those on the cheaper end, but usually don't come with the fizzy stuff. If you're more of a champagne champion, you'll have to spend a little more in the £100 area.

What size are the bottles of wine in advent calendars?

The size of the bottles varies depending on the calendar. Some offer mini bottles in the countdown to Christmas with a full-size bottle on Christmas Day, whereas others include a selection of full-size and mini bottles.

Whether the bottles are full-size or minis, it's a great way to try out some new wines at a seriously good price.

How to make a wine advent calendar

Making your own DIY wine advent calendar is actually pretty simple, but it could end up being quite expensive as you have to source the calendar itself and the bottles of wine.

DIY wine advent calendars can be a great investment, though, as you can reuse them, again and again, each year, but the best wine advent calendars feature never-seen-before bottles of wine from all over the world - so you're not limited to what you can find in the shops.
