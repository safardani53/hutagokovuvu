THE SOCIAL media personality has millions of subscribers and followers.

Niki DeMartino -- commonly known as Niki DeMar -- has found a great deal of success that began on YouTube.

Who is Niki DeMartino?

Niki DeMartino is a social media influencer, YouTuber, singer-songwriter, and actress.

She was born Nicola Teresa DeMartino on May 5, 1995, in Pennsylvania.

She rose to fame along with her twin sister, Gabi DeMartino.

The pair share a YouTube channel titled The Opposite Twins that has 9.47million subscribers.

Niki runs her own channel in which she has 2.37million subscribers.

She has also created a following beyond YouTube.

Niki has 3.9million followers on Instagram (@niki), 1.6 million followers on Twitter (@nikidemar), and 1.6 million followers on TikTok (@nikidemar).

In addition, Niki is largely known as a singer.

In 2021, she released the song Messy Room accompanied by a music video that received about 431,000 views.

In 2020, she released Alone In My Car along with a video that gained 1.4million views.

How did Niki DeMartino begin her career?

Before The Opposite Twins, Niki and Gabi started a channel called 00RemakeGirls that has approximately 210,000 subscribers.

Their older sister, Alex DeMartino, also held a role in this channel.

They created multiple parodies, including Spring Breakers Movie Parody and Keeping up with the Kardashians SPOOF "Touchy Subject" in 2013.

What are Niki DeMartino's acting roles and awards?

Niki has had acting roles in the 2016 Hulu horror show Freakish and the 2016 YouTube Red film Dance Camp.

She appeared in the 2015 YouTube horror short If Life Was a Scary Movie in which she co-wrote.

Niki has also made her mark on the fashion and beauty industries.

She won the 2018 Streamy Award for Best Fashion and served as a Savage x Fenty 2021 Global Ambassador.
