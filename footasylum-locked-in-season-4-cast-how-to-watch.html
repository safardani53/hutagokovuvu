FOOTASYLUM Locked In is back for a fourth season, with 10 content creators battling it out for a prize of £10,000.

Here we take a look at the cast, when it starts and how to tune in.

When does Footasylum season 4 start?

Series 4 of Footasylum Locked In has already started.

The first episode dropped on November 1, 2023.

The content creating contest lasts a fortnight, with the cast competing in various challenges to win the top prize of £10,000.

Who is in the cast of Footasylum season 4?

Ash Holme

Presenter, actress and content creator Ash Holme launched her TikTok in 2020.

Shannon Langdon

Shannon posts videos about lifestyle content, during which she often undertakes an array of challenges.

Fu Izzy

Fu Izzy is a Footasylum regular and fan favourite.

Madam Joyce

Madam Joyce is a radio and social media star known for her sense of humour and colourful personality.

Starplayer

Starplayer is a cousin of Footasylum veteran Chunkz.
