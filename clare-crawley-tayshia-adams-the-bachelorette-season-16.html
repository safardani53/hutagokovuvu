Who replaced Clare Crawley on The Bachelorette?

April 08, 2024
blog

CLARE Crawley was replaced by Tayshia Adams after five episodes on season 16 of The Bachelorette.

Tayshia stepped up to the plate after Clare fell in love with Bachelorette contestant Dale Moss and left the show.

Who replaced Clare Crawley on The Bachelorette?

For the first time in the Bachelor franchise’s two-decade history, the star of The Bachelorette was replaced.

Tayshia, 30, who was born on September 4, 1990, was a last-minute replacement after failing to find a longtime love on her previous Bachelor appearances.

Tayshia replaced Clare Crawley after Clare fell head-over-heels for contestant Dale Moss immediately when the season began filming in a bubble at La Quinta Resort & Club near Palm Springs, California.

Tayshia hails from Newport Beach, California, and worked as a phlebotomist before becoming a reality TV star. 

She was on season 23 of the Bachelor starring Colton Underwood.

Upon her elimination, she got to go on season six of Bachelor in Paradise in 2019 alongside other fan favorites. 

Why was Clare Crawley replaced?

Clare, 39, and Dale, 32, got engaged on The Bachelorette after nearly two weeks of filming. 

On the November 5 episode, fans watched Clare tell host Chris Harrison she wanted to end her journey with everyone on the show and tell Dale he was the one.

During a dinner date, Dale told Clare he was also falling in love with her, and the pair got engaged.

After leaving their Palm Springs resort together, the engaged couple shot intimate footage cooking together four days later.

According to reports, the production team "realized they had a problem on their hands" when sparks flew between the two.

Discussing the furor, Reality Steve claimed that producers "embraced" Clare's chemistry with Dale to use it as this season's "big twist" in light of the coronavirus pandemic limiting the show to one location.

The blogger said: "This was going to be a different, almost boring, season from the get go due to COVID, one location, no travel, etc.

"So instead of fighting Clare on this, they embraced it, and they’re using it as their big 'twist' this season."

Are Clare Crawley and Dale Moss still together?

Clare and Dale are still very much in love after their explosive show exit.

A source close to the couple exclusively told The Sun that the pair are house hunting, as they continue to live long distance. 

The insider said: “They are looking for a new place in Sacramento so Clare can be close to her mother. 

“Dale will continue living and working in NYC, and they will both travel to see one another. 

“They are doing great and are excited to spend the holidays together.”

The source continued the hair stylist and former football player are spending Thanksgiving week “with family and friends in La Jolla, California.”

The future spouses are “enjoying some quality time away with one another."

Dale was at the center of a cheating scandal when OK! Magazine reported that he was spotted in New York's SoHo area with three women on November 14.
