Who is Craig Robinson?

Born on October 25, 1971, Craig Robinson is a native of Chicago.

He grew up on the South Side, and later attended Whitney M Young Magnet High School.

In 1994, Robinson earned his Bachelor's degree from Illinois State University.

Robinson went on to receive a Master's degree in Education from Saint Xavier University.
