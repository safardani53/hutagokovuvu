Charlotte Church Age, Biography

Charlotte Church was born on February 21, 1986, to Stephen Reed and Maria Reed. She is from the Cardiff suburb of Llandaff. Her nationality is Welsh, and her ethnicity is Welsh-White. Her parents divorced when she was a child, and she was eventually adopted by her mother’s second husband, James Church. She has four siblings: two younger biological half-brothers, Luke and Alexander, and two older adoptive half-siblings, Elisha and Andrew, from her adoptive father’s previous marriage.

Her interest in singing was evident when she began singing publicly at the age of three and a half years at a family vacation camp. She attended The Cathedral School in Llandaff before receiving a vocal scholarship to Howell’s School in Llandaff in 1998. She used tutors to help her balance her schoolwork and singing career. As of 2023, she is 37 years old. She dropped out of school when she was sixteen.

Charlotte Church’s Husband

Charlotte Church was in a four-year relationship with rugby player Gavin Henson before they split up in 2010. He and she have two children. Ruby, their daughter, was born in September 2007, and Dexter, their son, was born in January 2009.

Church began dating musician Jonathan Powell later in 2010. She and Powell secretly married at a registered office in Cardiff on 4 October 2017, before blessing their marriage with an intimate ceremony in front of family and friends under a tree at their Dinas Powys home.

In August 2020, Church and Powell welcomed their first child, a daughter named Frida Simone.
