Wayne and Coleen Rooney head for Xmas dinner with family at plush restaurant

May 28, 2024
blog

WAYNE ROONEY and his wife Coleen enjoyed a Christmas dinner at Victors restaurant in Alderley Edge having returned to England for the holiday season.

Coleen's parents accompanied the Rooneys as they were seen heading to the plush restaurant without their four sons, Kai, Klay, Kit and Cass.

The brunette WAG looked stunning in her velvet polo neck with diamante studs, also highlighting her figure with black heels.

While Wayne opted for a casual attire and donned a bomber jacket with blue jeans and sneakers.

Relieved from parenting duties, the famous couple enjoyed their return to England as Coleen shared her joy with fans on social media.

She posted a picture on Instagram sitting alongside the D.C. United player and commented: "It’s so good to be home!!! Loved the new @victors_restaurants in Alderley Edge."

Victors serves a modern American menu where a truffle-seasoned rib-eye steak on the bone costs £56.

While a three-course Christmas menu featuring a whole grilled lobster and turkey ballotine costs £75 per person.

The Rooneys have returned to England following the end of the MLS season.

Wayne's D.C. United lost to Columbus Crew on penalties in the play-offs qualifiers at the beginning of November.
