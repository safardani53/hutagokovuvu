Bio:
Jordan Larson (born Jordan Quinn Larson in Fremont, Nebraska on October 16, 1986) is an American volleyball player who plays outside hitter. After playing volleyball for the University of Nebraska–Lincoln and graduating in 2008, Jordan began her professional volleyball career in 2009 with Russian club team Dynamo Kazan while also playing for the U.S. women’s national team. She was selected to play on the USA Women’s Olympic Volleyball Team in the 2012 Olympic Games in London, helping Team USA win the silver medal. Jordan was also part of the gold medal-winning U.S. team in the 2014 World Championship. She has been playing for Turkish volleyball club Eczacibasi VitrA since 2014, but was selected to once again join the USA Women’s Olympic Volleyball Team for the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio de Janeiro.

Best Known For:
Jordan is best known for playing outside hitter for the USA Women’s Olympic Volleyball Team and U.S. women’s national team.

Personal Life:
Jordan has been married to Luke Burbach in May 2009.
