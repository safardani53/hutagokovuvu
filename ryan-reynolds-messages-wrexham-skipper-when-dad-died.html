FILM star Ryan Reynolds sent a touching message to his Wrexham footie team’s skipper when his dad died after a brain op.

The League Two club’s co-owner tweeted to Ben Tozer: “Gut wrenching.

“Sending so much love to the Tozer family.”

Defender Ben, 33, revealed dad Keith, 59, was diagnosed with leukaemia on Saturday.

He needed the brain op the next day.

But despite it going “positively” Keith died on Monday.
