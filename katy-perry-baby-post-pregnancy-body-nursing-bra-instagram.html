KATY Perry has shown fans her post-baby body in a nursing bra just five days after giving birth to her daughter, Daisy Dove.

Katy, 35, took to Instagram on Sunday evening to poke fun at the fact that she wasn't glammed up for the 2020 MTV Video Music Awards.

For the first time ever, the annual ceremony took place virtually and although celebrities couldn't attend the VMAs in person, it didn't stop them from hitting the green screen step-and-repeat in style.

In the down-to-earth snap, the pop star is wearing a flesh-colored nursing bra used with a breast pump, and a pair of comfy-looking grey knickers, which were pulled halfway over her tummy.

She decided to pair the photo with her single 'Not The End Of The World' and added a camera gif too.

Katy is stood in her bathroom, make-up free and looks rather sleepy - no doubt due to the sleepless nights from having a newborn baby.

The Part Of Me singer even tagged her hair and make-up as being done by @exhaustion.

She wore her blonde hair in a low, messy ponytail as she apparently prepared to pump to feed her little girl.

Katy and her fiancé Orlando Bloom welcomed their daughter, Daisy Dove, last week and announced the birth on social media with an adorable photo.

In the black and white snap, Katy and 43-year-old Orlando can be seen clutching their little girl's tiny hand just hours after welcoming her into the world.

But despite the new arrival, hard-working Katy has still been promoting her new album, Smile, posting on Twitter the same day she announced Daisy's birth: "delivering a baby and a record in the same week, dont f*** with mama aka #whatmakesawoman #smile"

It was recently revealed that the couple will introduce their little one to Orlando's ex-wife Miranda Kerr - whom he shares his nine-year-old son Flynn with.

A source told Hollywood Life: "Katy and Orlando are enjoying their time with their daughter right now so visitors are limited.

"Katy’s family has been there with her but other than that they’re pretty well in a cocoon."

The insider said that Katy has "been in touch with all of her friends and as soon as she’s ready for visits Katy will welcome Miranda with open arms."

The Australian model married Snapchat co-founder Evan Spiegel in 2017 and the couple share two sons together.

Miranda, 37, even commented on the couple's baby picture and wrote: "I'm so happy for you guys. Can't wait to meet her."

Katy – who was close to being 10 months pregnant – and Orlando announced the birth of their daughter on Thursday with a statement on the Unicef website, as they are both Goodwill Ambassadors to the global organization.

The parents gushed: “We are floating with love and wonder from the safe and healthy arrival of our daughter.

"But we know we’re the lucky ones and not everyone can have a birthing experience as peaceful as ours was," they said of tough times in the wake of the coronavirus pandemic.
