How tall is Pete Davidson?

Pete Davidson was one of the taller members of the Saturday Night Live cast.

Viewers have been wondering what the actor's height is for years.

According to multiple media outlets, Davidson stands tall at a striking 6-foot-3 inches.

Davidson, himself, has made jokes about his long limbs on a number of SNL episodes, once saying: "I kind of look like I should be outside a car dealership flapping in the wind."

Is Pete Davidson still on Saturday Night Live?

Pete Davidson got his foot in the door with television appearances on various MTV shows, including Failosophy and Guy Code.

In 2014, Davidson's career quickly took off after he joined SNL, becoming the youngest-ever cast member.

The following year, Davidson was asked to participate in the Comedy Central Roast of Justin Bieber. This only accelerated his fame as he was cracking jokes alongside well-known comedians, including Hannibal Buress and Chris D'elia.

Davidson is also an actor and has appeared in many films and television shows.

He made his acting debut on Brooklyn Nine-Nine in 2013 and starred in movies like Big Time Adolescence and The Suicide Squad.

In 2016, Davidson appeared on the Forbes 30 Under 30 list. The same year, he filmed his first stand-up comedy special on Comedy Central.
