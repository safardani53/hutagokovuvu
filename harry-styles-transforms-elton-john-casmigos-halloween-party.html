HARRY Styles transformed into Elton John last night as put on a shimmering LA Dodgers uniform for the star-studded Casamigos Halloween party in Beverly Hills.

The singer, 24, looked to be having a blast as the iconic musician in his pomp, replicating Elton's attire from his legendary shows at Dodger Stadium in 1975.

His silver and blue kit glistened as did his pink square shades as he stuck his tongue out and provided an open-mouth smile for the camera.

He joined reality star Paris Hilton at the celeb bash, with the hotel heiress impossible to miss in her furry ears and crop top and platform boots.

P Diddy looked suave in a pilot's outfit complete with a peaked cap, and the Gerber family also took their styling very seriously.

Rande dressed as Ziggy Stardust complete with red hair and lightning bolt face paint.
