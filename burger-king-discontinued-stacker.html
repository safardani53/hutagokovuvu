Four items you'll no longer see at Burger King including one 'hand-breaded' sandwich - and customers

March 30, 2024
blog

FAST foodies are constantly dealing with the stress of their favorite items leaving menus.

While it's natural for companies to test things, it’s also likely that some of your favorites will be long gone.

Over the year, Burger King fans have said so long to favorites like the Mac' n Cheetos - macaroni and cheese bites deep-fried with a crispy coating of the original brand's cheese powder.

However, in order to keep customers happy, the burger joint does occasionally bring items back - but with a twist.

This includes the American Original Chicken Sandwich and the Italian Original Chicken Sandwich returning to menus earlier this year along with its new Mexican Original Chicken Sandwich.

Although, below we focus on the menu items that customers are begging to return.

1. STACKERS

These burgers were first launched in 2006 and quickly became a favorite with hungry customers.

The stackers were offered in three sizes: single, double, and triple.

Plus, the burgers had no frills - just three simple ingredients meat, cheese, and bacon.

Fans liked them for the flavor and the price the single was $1 the double was $2, and the triple was $3.

When the Stackers left fans were outraged.

One Twitter user wrote: "Burger king discontinued the stacker, looks like I'm never eating there again."

Then, just this year, the burger joint added it once more only to quietly remove it once again.

2. CH'KING

The Ch'king was only on menus for a short amount of time but made a big impact.

The fan favorite had hand-breaded chicken with mustard, bacon, mayonnaise, and savory sauce, among other ingredients.

The Ch'king came in a regular offering and a spicy version.

After a little over a year, Burger King decided to remove it over the summer.

This did not sit well with customers as one Twitter user wrote: "BURGER KING DISCONTINUED THEIR CH’KINGS. ABSOLUTELY DO NOT HIT ME UP. IM DEVASTATED."

3. JALAPEÑO CHEDDAR BITES

The Jalapeño Cheddar Bites hit menus in 2020 and quickly grew in popularity.

They had gooey cheddar cheese and spicy jalapeño pieces covered in a light, crispy coating.

While the company did say these would only be around for a limited time, fans did not prepare themselves for their absence.

4. FRIES

Burger King is known for getting creative with its fries offerings.

The fast-food chain has created Chicken Fries, Spicy Chicken Fries, the Satisfries, and much more.

One in particular really stung when they left and that was the Funnel Cake Fries.

These launched in 2009 but were discontinued quickly after.

After much outcry, a decade later the burger joint put them back on the menu where customers could grab a pack of nine pieces at $1.99 in 2019.

Unfortunately, the item has since gone once more.

Although, Burger King did offer two new kinds of fries to its menu in February - Mozzarella Fries and Churro Fries.

The new Mozzarella Fries and new Churro Fries will be trial runs, so don't get too attached yet.

The breaded, fry-shaped mozzarella sticks are fried and crispy and served with two marinara dipping sauces.
