Take that haters! Outspoken starlet Danielle Fishel let her fit physique do the talking on Oct. 27 when she stepped out of the ocean, debuting her stunning bikini body while on her honeymoon with new husband Tim Belusko in Hawaii. 

The Girl Meets World star, 32, recently took to Twitter after critics bashed her weight at her Oct. 19 wedding. 

“Ppl saying I was FAT @ my wedding: u r the worst kind of ppl on the PLANET. I weigh 107 pounds & am 5’1″. YOU are the reason anorexia exists,” she tweeted on Oct. 22. “I hope you’ll look at your own miserable lives and learn to stop judging others on their weight and looks. Love and happiness wins again.”

This isn’t Fishel’s first time showing some skin. The actress appeared in cream-colored lingerie on the April 2013 cover of Maxim magazine.
