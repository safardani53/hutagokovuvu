Snapchat Memories will FINALLY let you keep all those sexy naked selfies

February 29, 2024
blog

Snapchat is famous for letting users transmit pictures of their unmentionables across the world, safe in the knowledge these naughty images would self-destruct.

But anyone who was looking for a bit more of a permanent relationship with their sordid selfies was basically out of luck - until now.

The photo-sharing app has unveiled a new way of keeping pictures and short films, whilst making sure the really naughty ones aren't seen by the wrong person.

This selfie storage facility is called Memories and is "a personal collection of your favourite moments", assuming you've managed to record these moments on Snapchat in the first place.

It is now being rolled out across the world and is perhaps the most significant update to Snapchat that's ever been released.

In the video above, Snapchat uses two pretty young blonde things to demonstrate why Memories is useful.

The couple are showing pictures of their paradise holiday to her Dad, when suddenly it cuts to a graphic shot of her bum.

She snatches back the phone and moves the image to her "my eyes only folder", although she probably should have spared her father's blushes by doing this before dinner.

"We realised that Snapchatters want to feel comfortable showing their Memories to friends while they’re hanging out together, so we made it easy to move Snaps and Stories to My Eyes Only — and avoid awkward moments when a friend stumbles upon a Snap meant just for you," the firm wrote.

If you want to access memories, just swipe up from the camera on Snapchat.

You'll be able to search through Memories and build new stories based on what you find.

Snapchat will send you a (non-naked) snap when it's your time to start building Memories.
