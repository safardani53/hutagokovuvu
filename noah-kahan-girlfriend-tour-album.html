Who is Noah Kahan?

Kahan is a singer-songwriter known for releasing songs in the folk-infused pop genre.

He was born on January 1, 1977, and hails from Stafford, Vermont.

In 2017, Kahan released multiple singles that were later part of his 2019 album, Busyhead.

He has since released two additional albums, I Was / I Am (2021) and Stick Seasons (2023).
