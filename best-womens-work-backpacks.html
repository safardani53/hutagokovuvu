What features should I look for in a work bag?

The features of your work bag are highly dependent on your job and your habits.

You might need to carry a laptop, notepad or water bottle; in that case, it's best to choose a backpack with a padded laptop pocket and additional storage space.

But if all you need is your purse and your phone, you might get away with a stylish smaller model.

Some backpacks are also specifically designed for runners or for those who run-commute.

On hectic days like these, you need as many pockets and dividers in your bag as possible, to ensure easy access and organisation for those flustered and stressful moments.

Are backpacks better for your back?

The main benefit of using a backpack is that it balances out the weight on your shoulders, making it easier to carry your stuff.

That way, there isn't loads of pressure on just one side of your body, giving you better posture and generally better back-health.

You'll also find that you are able to carry a heavier load, because the weight is being split in two across your body, rather than weighing down one side with a huge handbag.
