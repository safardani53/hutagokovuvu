KIM Kardashian is facing scrutiny over her lavish birthday gift for her son, Psalm after sharing a photo of the tot sporting pricey jewelry.

The 41-year-old celebrated her youngest child's third birthday with an outlandish Hulk-themed party.

In a tribute to her baby boy's special day on Monday, Kim shared a snap of the tot wearing a giant emerald chain on her Instagram Stories.

The pic focused on Psalm's new neck attire, which included a giant studded letter "P," initialing the little one's unique first name.

In a second snap, Kim, who shares her son with her ex-husband Kanye West, added the caption, "PERIODDDDDDDD!!!!!" again, emphasizing the "P" in the word.

According to Ajay Anand, CEO of Rare Carat Diamond Marketplace, the price was no concern to the SKIMS founder as the piece is reportedly worth $200K.

The jewelry designer exclusively told The Sun that the beauty appeared to "contain over 40 carats of emeralds" of square-shaped and rectangular cuts.

"Often, jewelry designers will resort to using smaller emerald sizes in the chain to keep prices down; it is clear that no expense was spared here on quite a chunky design," said Ajay.

The diamond expert also pointed out the uniqueness of the accessory, adding: "What is unusual and not as exclusive about this necklace is that it seems to combine different emerald shades.

"Usually, high-end jewelry would stay consistent to one shade and one country of origin. Perhaps that is a sign that their jeweler is cutting a few corners."

The Kardashian fan Instagram account, Kardashian Social, also took their take on the expensive gift, pointing out that Kim had spoiled her son with expensive neckwear in the past.

Along with the photo the Hulu star shared of Psalm wearing his sparkly present, they posted a throwback video of Kim boasting about an enormous gold chain the youngster previously wore.

In the clip from an interview on The Ellen DeGeneres Show, Kim corrected the talk show host who called her son's sizeable jewelry "fake."

"He wears this fake, giant chain -" Ellen began to describe the tot's wardrobe before Kim cut her off: "Wait, did you say fake?"

The Finding Dory star paused in disbelief as Kim giggled at her reaction.

The pair continued to debate the authenticity of the jewelry as Kim insisted the chain was "not" fake.

The 64-year-old joked: "Oh my god, it's huge! How can he keep his head up? It looks like a wrestler's belt or something... I just assumed because it was so huge, it's hilarious."

One follower laughed at the interaction, commenting: "Kim was so offended" with a laughing face emoji.

A second person gushed over the young boy's lavish gift, writing: "wow what a lucky kid but it looks good."
