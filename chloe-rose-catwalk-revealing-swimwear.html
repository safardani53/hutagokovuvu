Models show off their amazing bums and under-boobs on Chloe Rose's VERY revealing swimwear collectio

February 21, 2024
blog

THIS summer, it's been impossible to avoid the extreme underboob bikini trend or the racy high leg swimsuits which have taken our social media feeds by storm.

And it looks like these divisive designs are here to stay as a VERY revealing new swimswear collection has just hit the catwalk at Miami Swim Week.

American designer Chloe Rose debuted her latest swimwear range in Miami over the weekend.

Taking inspiration from the Love Island underboob trend, the designer also paid tribute to the bafflingly high-cut bikini bottom trend which divided the internet earlier this year.

And while most of us would either pair a more revealing top with some high-coverage bottoms (or vice versa), the swimwear combined both these racy trends in her one-piece costumes.

Harking back to 80s Baywatch, Chloe Rose's swimsuits featured an extremely high-cut leg as well as a gaping cut-out under the boobs and over the torso.

Not to mention how each of the designs are bound to show off a generous amount of sideboob on the beach,

In other words, these cossies are the very definition of "poolside posing" and don't exactly serve the most practical purpose.

What's more, even the designer's range of bikinis featured a range of rather uncomfortable cuts.

While one leopard-print bikini had an equally high-cut bottom half, another was made entirely of our grandma's favourite tweed.

But the one design we could see ourselves modelling on our next holiday? The pretty pink tartan bikini and skirt which wouldn't look out of place in Cher Horowitz's wardrobe.

This isn't the first time the Miami Swim Week catwalk has graced us with some weird and wonderful designs.

Last year, stick-on swimwear became the talking point of the event as models were sent down the catwalk in Duct-Tape bikinis.

Leaving equally little to the imagination, these startling pieces of "swimwear" were effectively made up of a strip of tape covering the nipple and crotch area.

Meanwhile models at the Sports Illustrated show in Miami this year also put on a daring display in their range of swimwear...
