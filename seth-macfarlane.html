Seth MacFarlane is an American actor, singer, songwriter, and screenwriter who is best for producing, writing, and animating the hit TV show .

Seth MacFarlane Age, Height, Childhood, Education

Seth was born in Kent, Connecticut to parents Ronald Milton MacFarlane and Ann Perry on 26 October 1973. He has a sister named Rachael Ann MacFarlane who is a voice actress. Seth showed interest in animation and cartoons from an early age and used to draw cartoon characters like Fred Flintstone, and Woody Woodpecker when he was just 2.

Seth was enrolled in the Rhode Island School of Design to study animation and video from where he earned a bachelor's degree in Fine Arts. His early interests were working for Disney, but his thesis film The Life of Larry eventually got him an offer to work with Hanna-Barbera.
