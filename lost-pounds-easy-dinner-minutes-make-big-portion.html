I lost 70 lbs - my easy dinner takes just 10 minutes to make and it was such a big portion

June 07, 2024
blog

A TIKTOKER has revealed her go-to easy dinner after losing a whopping 70 pounds.

She claimed her simple recipe helped her on her weight loss journey.

Caroline Mabeus (@carolinemabeus) filmed herself in the kitchen preparing the meal in a video on TikTok.

"Alright, make dinner with me," she said. "70 pounds down. Let's do it."

She filmed herself prepping ingredients from the grocery store to make the dinner.

"I love these pre-marinated chicken options from Aldi. I did the southwest style one," she revealed.

She added carbohydrates and fiber to make it a well-rounded meal.

"I paired it with broccoli and basmati rice, topped it with this mango salsa," she said.

She shared that her easy recipe is not only delicious, but it's also low in calories.

"It was so good and 500 calories," she said.

She raved about the chicken dinner in the caption of the video.

"Make dinner with me," she said. "This was so good and only took like 10 minutes. It was so good and it was such a big portion."

The video came as part of a trend where people show off their go-to recipes.

With a desire to get in shape, many influencers are detailing their own fitness journeys.

Many people are proving that eating healthy doesn't have to take hours.

The lazy dinners are here to stay as people look for simpler ways to eat better.
