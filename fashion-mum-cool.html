I'm fashion pro &amp; what mums should be wearing at the school gates - it will stop you looking boring

February 21, 2024
blog

BECOMING a mum is a whole new game to life. 

Learning how to look after your kids is one thing, but getting your mojo back is another.

It’s not impossible and everything takes time. 

And one place some mums like to start is their wardrobe. 

Holley, who goes by the name of shopholleygirl on TikTok, is here to reveal her fashion tricks to dress like “a cool mum”. 

She claims that everyone should own a black T-shirt dress.

She says: “Everyone needs a black T-shirt dress in their wardrobe.

"It is such a staple piece, it’s amazing in the summer with sandals.

"It’s perfect for vacation, you can also style it with sneakers and boots with layers and jackets. 

“You get the idea.”

Most read in Fabulous

Holley then styles the T-shirt with a light blue cotton shirt.

She says: “And you might normally put on a cardigan over something like this. 

“But instead of that, we are actually going to do this button down (buttoned shirt), nice soft colour and it has a very relaxed, soft, boyfriend style fit to it. 

“I’m going to roll my sleeves, and we’re really just going to wear this like you would wear a cardigan,

“But doing it with a button down is going to change the whole look.”

Holley then paired it with sneakers to “keep it really casual and comfortable, and accessorising with a little bit of jewellery is really going to pull this together.” 

She then adds Bobby sunglasses and a Zoey bag. 

Holley continues: “It is such a comfortable fit, you might not normally think to put a button down or wear a dress like this.

"But you can see how it totally updates your look and it takes this T-shirt dress, and it gives it a whole new look.

“This would be perfect for vacation, and you could also swap this (trainer) for a sandal in the summer. 

“These are all staple pieces that you can mix and match in other ways as well to really help you to make the most out of your wardrobe.”

Many people rushed to the comments section to share their thoughts. 

One wrote: “Thanks for sharing, now I know how to wear accessories with my T-shirt dress.” 

Another person wasn’t comfortable with the look, and wrote: “My love handles said no ma’am.”
