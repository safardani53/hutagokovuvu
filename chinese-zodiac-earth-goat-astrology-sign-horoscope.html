IN the Chinese zodiac, the Earth Goat represents those born in 1979.

Chinese astrology is based on animals and how their characteristics embody the year you were born. 

What year is the Earth Goat?

Chinese zodiac signs identify birth years with certain animals including the Rat, the Ox, the Tiger, the Rabbit, the Dragon, the Snake, the Horse, the Goat, the Monkey, the Rooster, the Dog and the Pig.

The Goat is the eighth animal in the 12-year cycle and is also given to those born in 1931, 1943, 1955, 1967, 1979, 1991, 2003, and 2015.

The Earth Goat is a sub-component of the Goat in the Chinese zodiac. 

Each zodiac sign has its own element: Earth, Wood, Fire, Metal and Water, which add more uniqueness and characteristics to the sign.
