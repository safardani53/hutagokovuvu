Kathy Hilton is a socialite and ‘friend’ of the ‘Real Housewives’ franchise. See her best photos here.

Kathy Hilton, born March 13, 1959, is an American socialite, fashion designer, philanthropist and television personality, married to hotel heir Rick Hilton in 1979 when she was 20-years-old. She is the mother of socialite Paris Hilton, fashion designer Nicky Hilton, real estate developer Barron Hilton, and youngest Conrad Hilton III. She is the sister of ‘The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills’ stars Kim Richards and Kyle Richards, and later would appear on the Bravo series herself. She also hosted the 2005 reality show ‘I Want To Be a Hilton’ on NBC and appeared in Paris’ 2011 series ‘The World According to Paris’ and 2021’s ‘Paris In Love’.
