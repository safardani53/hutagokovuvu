Who is the tallest Kardashian/Jenner sister? Here is how Kim, Kendall and Kylie measure up

January 28, 2024
blog

KOURTNEY Kardashian's a pint-sized beauty, while her little sister Kendall Jenner is a striking catwalk model.

So who's the tallest of the five sisters, and where does mum Kris measure up? Here's what we know...

Who's the tallest Kardashian-Jenner sister?

Kendall Jenner: 5ft10

Dad Caitlin is 6ft2, so it's hardly surprising her daughters are tall too.

Kendall, 22, towers over her 'big' sisters - with the exception of Khloe - with her height paving her way for a future in modelling.

Kendall Jenner at Cannes in ANOTHER see-through dress

Khloe Kardashian: 5ft10

Khloe, who's now a mum-of-one to baby True, matches Kendall at 5ft10.

Her height may explain why the 34-year-old has a thing for basketball players.

Speaking about Khloe and Kim, she previously joked to Us Weekly: "They are 5ft and 5ft2, so I look massive next to them.

"Everyone expects me to be 9ft tall...when they meet me."

Kylie Jenner: 5ft6

Mum-of-one Kylie comes in the middle of the pack - measuring in at 5ft6.

The 21-year-old previously revealed her height in a tweet to a fan.

Fun fact: she's the same height as momager Kris.

Kim Kardashian: 5ft2

Mum-of-three Kim, 37, is hardly ever seen without a pair of sky high stilettos, and there's a reason for that.

She measures in at 5ft2, while hubby Kanye West is also shorter than Khloe and Kendall - at 5ft9.
