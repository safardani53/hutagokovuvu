American television personality Angelina Pivarnick never had the flair to maintain a successful relationship throughout her romance history but this time seemed a bit different as she effused about her fiance's romantic proposal.

Angelina Pivarnick is quite known for starring in the first two seasons of the MTV reality show, Jersey Shore and VH1 show, Couples Therapy.

Angelina after a long hiatus made her return to television in Jersey Shore: Family Vacation that premiered globally in April 2018.

Angelina Pivarnick’s Bio: Age & Career In Television, Songwriting

Born on 26 June 1986, Angelina Pivarnick was raised in Staten Island, New York. She started her career in television after appearing in the reality television series, Jersey Shore. She was evicted in the third episode of season one from the shore house after refusing to work her shift at the T-shirt shop.

The 32-year-old media personality appeared again on the second season of the show in Miami Beach, Florida and departed the house after violent confrontations with cast-members Nicole ‘Snooki’ Polizzi and Michael 'The Situation' Sorrentino.

After Jersey Shore, Angelina appeared in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling and in 2010, she released her song, I’m Hot. She had a brief appearance in the music video for Dacav5’s song, Dirty Style in 2011.

Her second song, Gotta Go Out featuring Tony Hanson ‘Felix’, that was released in August 2011 charted at #37 on the Billboard Dance/Club Play Songs list. Prior to her return on TV in2018, she also worked as an EMT in the big apple.

Angelina Pivarnick Engaged Twice But Failed To Get Married On Both Occasions

The Jersey Shore star has a history of romantic involvements, and they sure do not linger for a long time. Her first boyfriend to come into the spotlight was the policeman, Paul Imperiale who patrolled the beaches of the show. The affair ended after Paul was reassigned far from the Jersey Shore set.

And in 2011, Angelina introduced her newest engaged boyfriend David Kovacs, but their romance went downhill after rumors about Angelina cheating on David surfaced around. Despite claims of her innocence and pregnancy at the time, her 'boyfriend turned fiance' broke off the engagement. And it wasn’t long before she suffered a miscarriage, which as she later revealed was one of the hardest episodes in her life.

It was in 2012 when Angelina appeared in Couples Therapy with her new boyfriend, Chris Nirschel but their romance lasted just till the series’ ending. After a few years, Angelina was yet again engaged to Louie Gero in September 2016, and while she didn’t hesitate to share her excitement, the two got separated the same year due to some unrevealed reasons.

Angelina Is Engaged Again, Newest Fiance Is Romanticist

After going through some fickle and unsuccessful relationships, Angelina got engaged for the third time to her boyfriend, Chris Larangeira on 12 January 2018.

The two have only been dating for over a year, but they go way back with their friendship of 14 years. The husband-to-be proposed to Angelina in the most romantic way possible. She shared the experience to Us Weekly revealing that her boyfriend brought her back to his house where he had put flower petals lettering out ‘I Love You, Angelina,’ and she followed the trail of rose petals to his bed where there was a heart with the initials ‘A&C’ at the end.

Chris got down on one knee and put a ring on Angelina with perfect timing and emotion.

Chris had Angelina’s best friend, Adam Barta to help with the proposal and Adam confessed on Us Weekly that he hadn’t seen Angelina so happy and that he almost teared up at the scene.
