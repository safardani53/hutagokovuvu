Best eyebrow kits 2022 to shape, define and fill your brows

March 19, 2024
blog

EYEBROW maintenance needn't take ages in the salon primping and preening.

A top-rated eyebrow kit will transform unruly hairs and see you being your own "browtician" in no time.

The rise of the eyebrows as a key beauty trend may have been started by Cara Delevingne's bushy brows back in the 2010s.

But brows remain a mainstay for many beauty stans - and a key part of their makeup routine.

A premium kit should have everything you need to pluck, shape, define, fill and set your eyebrows.

Precision brushes and pigmented and long-lasting tints and gels are necessary for creating that on-trend bold brow.

There are endless eyebrow kits on the market, ranging from basic starter sets, to pro kits with all the bells-and-whistles.

If you need a hand figuring out the best eyebrow kit for you, check out our roundup below and never be brow-beaten again.

Read more:

1. Wonder Blading Brow Kit

Love the microbladed brow look? This kit from Wonderskin gives you the perfect finish without the costly salon visit.

The simple-to-use process allows you to swipe on the pigmented mask product, spray the activator and wipe access to reveal smudge-proof perfect brows.

The totally vegan and cruelty-free formula is 100% waterproof, so you don't have to worry about transfer or fading throughout the day.

  • Wonder Blading Brow Kit, £35 from Wonderskin - buy here

3. Benefit The Great Brow Basics Kit

New to brow-shading? This kit was made for you.

Start off by filling in gaps, shaping and defining with your Goof Proof Brow Pencil.

Then, grab your Gimme Brow+ Volumising Eyebrow Gel and simply brush the tip over your brow - the tint will grab on to hairs and create a fuller-looking finish.

Finally, use your Precisely, My Eyebrow Pencil to fill in any blanks with light "feather" strokes.

Use the integrated spoolie (little bristle tip that looks like a mascara) to blend for a professional finish.

It also comes in various shades to match your colouring.  A brilliant easy-to-use product that lasts for months.

  • Benefit The Great Brow Basics Kit, £30.15 from Lookfantastic - buy here

3. Anastasia Beverly Hills Brow Kit

No eyebrow product round-up is complete without the OG Anastasia Beverly Hills, making an appearance.

Created by the brow queen herself, Anastasia Soare, the kit features all of the brand's hero brow products in one place.

Perfect professional-looking arches using the Brow Wizz slim-line pencil tool which comes with spoolie brush on the end and clear brow gel.

Polished brows in no time. Finish up with a swipe of the mini Lash Brag mascara on your lashes to make your eyes pop. #Winning.

  • Anastasia Beverly Hills Brow Kit, £34 from Cult Beauty - buy here

4. Glossier Boy Brow + Brow Flick Duo

If natural looking brows are your thing, the Glossier Boy Brow and Brow Flick combo is a winner.

Everyone who has followed the meteoric rise of Glossier will know the sheer magic of Boy brow.

The lightweight gel formula works wonders at holding hairs in place, and it comes in a range of shades to suit all complexions.

The Brow Flick is a newer addition to the range, but no less loved. Ideal for creating precision shape and definition to your brows without them looking block-y.

  • Boy Brow + Brow Flick Duo, £26 from Glossier - buy here

5. Sleek MakeUP Brow Kit

This handbag-friendly set is a brill little take-anywhere product for out-and-about touch-ups.

First, sort out any stray hairs with the mini tweezers provided.

Use the angled brush to apply the pigmented wax in strokes going in the same direction as your natural hair. This will shape and define your brows before using the round brush to fill in any gaps and blend with the setting powder.

A really nifty little number that comes in four different shades, from Light to Extra Dark, at a great price. Browvo!

  • Sleek Make-up Brow Kit, £8.49 from Feel Unique - buy here

6. Urban Decay Brow Box

This kit is literally everything you need to curate gorgeous brows in one little travel-friendly set.

Don't let its small size fool you - each set includes two shades of ultra-fine, silky brow powder; a perfecting/setting wax (infused with nourishing aloe, beeswax and coconut); high-quality metal mini tweezers that get even the peskiest of tiny hairs; a mini angled brow brush for defined shaping; a mini spoolie for blending; and two mirrors, including one that magnifies so you can get up close and personal with your handiwork.

Comes in six different shades so you can choose which best suits your colouring.

Top tip? Blend the two powders together to really colour-match to your brows.

  • Urban Decay Brow Box, £22 from John Lewis - buy here

7. Collection Eyebrow Kit

Tiny price, big impact! This kit has received rave reviews, and it really is a bit of a wonder-buy.

It comes with three highly-pigmented powder shades (so you don't need to apply a lot to achieve a good result and it will last for ages), a dual-ended angled and blending brush and a clear setting brow gel.

Use the angled brush to shape your brows and fill in gaps, mixing the shades to get the perfect colour if needed.

Blend with the round end of the brush and then apply the brow gel, which will set the whole look and ensure it doesn't budge all day long.

  • Collection Eyebrow Kit Brunette, £4.49 from Boots - buy here

8. Refy Brow Collection

Refy is the brow brand everyone is talking about on social media.

Created by influencer and media personality, Jess Hunt, the brand offers some seriously good brow taming products.

If you're a fan of bolder brows (like Jess is know for) then you'll love using the Brow Sculpt, Brow Pomade and Brow Pencil that comes in the full kit.

The pomade is ideal for taming unruly hairs that never seem to lay down the way you want.

The brow sculpt tools and pencil allow you to build up definition and shape, for a long-lasting quality finish.

  • Refy Brow Collection, £40 from Selfridges - buy here
