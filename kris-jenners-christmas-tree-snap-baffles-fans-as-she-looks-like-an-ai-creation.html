Kris Jenners Christmas tree snap baffles fans as she looks like an AI creation Fri 15

blog

Kris Jenner’s Christmas tree is the star of the show – supposedly – but she posted what looks like an “AI generated photo” for her annual festive snap. Jenner showed off one of her ideal Christmas gifts, a pair of Alo sneakers, and fans are divided over the blinding white shoes. The stock photo vibe eerily comes not too long after that strange virtual set-up in daughter Khloe Kardashian’s house.

The queen of Christmas, AKA reality TV star Kris Jenner, is officially smiling in front of the tree for her yearly festive photoshoot. She’s getting her Christmas gifts for grandchildren and children ready, but not without first showing off those Alo white sneakers before they get worn in. There’s something strange about the photos that Kris Jenner’s social media fans have quickly picked up on AI vibes.

Kris Jenner’s Christmas tree theme

Kris Jenner’s Christmas tree has a fun red theme this year with a variety of different baubles, including lots of mini Santa Clauses, red ribbons, festive characters – the work of Australian contemporary artist Kathy Temin – and heart-shaped decorations. She also has a pink and purple tree decorated with sweet treat ornaments on display in her home.

She first debuted her tree in November 2023, but also has Corey and Kris stockings hung up above the fireplace, with lots of pine hanging over the side to frame the cosy area. The mansion even has huge reindeer ornaments and to be a little bit extra, a mini merry-go-round statue!

A massive gingerbread house, more pine and another huge Santa ornament covers her kitchen sides. That’s not all, as Father Christmas himself sits next to her tree with his sack and teddy!

Snap looks like ‘AI generated photo’

When Kris Jenner finally uploaded the Christmas tree snaps we’ve all been waiting for, it was the tree that caught our eye. But that’s not all, as those white Alo trainers are blinding.

Plus, the editing of the photo seems almost far too perfect. One fan commented: “Why does this feel like an AI generated photo or a stock photo.” Fellow commenters had the same thought process.

Another penned: “What in the stock photos is this?” When it came to the white trainers, a follower wrote: “I don’t know about those giant white teenage shoes but I love the rest of your outfit.”
