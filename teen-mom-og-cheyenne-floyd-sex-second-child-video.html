Teen Mom OGs Cheyenne Floyd Reveals Sex Of Second Child: Watch Hollywood Life

It’s a boy! Cheyenne Floyd, 28, has revealed she will welcome a baby boy with Zach Davis in 2021. The Teen Mom OG star, who has a three-year-old daughter Ryder with her ex Cory Wharton, revealed the exciting news on New Year’s Day. “I might still be in shock that I am having a boy. Happiness is an understatement to try to explain how we feel. My family and I are elated,” she captioned her January 1 Instagram video. “Ryder wanted a baby brother, and swears she knew this whole time. I know she’s going to be an amazing big sister. Mommy and daddy love you & can’t wait to meet you.”

The reality star also noted that everyone in the crowd at her gender reveal party “quarantined and was tested 3-4 times before attending our gender reveal to make sure we were being as safe as possible.” In the sweet video, the parents-to-be posed in front of a “Here for the Sex” balloon wall, as she showed off her baby bump in an off-the-shoulder pink dress and matching sandals. The MTV star first revealed that she was pregnant with her second child on December 17. She shared a video, with her new baby bump in tow, strolling along a beach with Zach and her mini-me daughter. “Coming soon 2021,” Cheyenne simply captioned the video. She gave her 1.3 million Instagram followers more details about her pregnancy, explaining that she prayed for a second child. “We are extremely blessed and honored that this little one has chosen us as parents,” Cheyenne wrote underneath a photo of Ryder sweetly kissing her pregnant mom’s belly. “We prayed for this moment and wanted to hold on to it as long as we could. The past few months have been life changing in the best ways possible.”

She continued, “Ryder is so excited to be a big sister again and is already super protective over my bump. She prays for the baby every night and kisses my belly every morning. Zach, you have been nothing but supportive and there every step of the way. Thinking about how young we were when we first met and how far we have come, I could not be more proud of us today. My heart is so full. Let the next adventure began.”
