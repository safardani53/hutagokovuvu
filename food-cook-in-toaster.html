Think your toaster is just for bread? You're wrong... here are eight other foods you can cook in it

June 04, 2024
blog

SOME people love to spend hours on home-cooked meals, but there are always days when time is scarce.

Anything to speed up the process is ideal, which is where your toaster comes in.

That's because it can cook a lot more than just bread. Here are some ideas for other ways to put it to use.

1. Potato waffles

No need to spend time waiting for the grill to heat up, you can have delicious golden brown waffles in minutes by popping them in the toaster.
What's more, it even works straight from frozen

2. Veggie burgers

You must never cook beef burgers in the toaster because so much fat seeps out of them it would be dangerous, but it's a different story with the veggie variety.

They will come out crisp and no clean up is required.

3. Sweet potato

Cut down on the washing up by popping your sweet potato in the toaster.

Chop it up into centimetre-wide slices and toast it for around 10-15 minutes and you're good to go.

4. Leftover pizza

You've probably tried reheating your day-old pizza in the microwave, but it can get soggy.

Use a toaster bag to warm it in the toaster and you'll avoid mess too.

5. Crispy bacon

Yes, you heard right, you can cook crispy bacon in the toaster.

It's quick, easy and fuss free when you use a toaster bag - try it today for the fastest way to a bacon sarnie.

6. Cheese toastie

Assemble your sandwich and pop it in a toaster bag for a quick snack that tastes as good as it looks.

Just two or three minutes is all it takes to create this delicious meal.

7. Tortilla chips

This easy tortilla chip idea uses wraps or flour tortillas, so it's great if you have leftovers too.

Simply toast them for a few minutes before breaking them apart to make smaller crisps and serving them up with dip.

8. Roasted asparagus

A quick side dish that takes minutes in the toaster bag, throw in some olive oil and paprika with a handful of asparagus for this delicious veggie dish.
