Is Felisha Terrell Married? Inside Her Love Life with Donae Burston

April 14, 2024
blog

Felisha Terrell is a film and television actress who has acted in several acting projects, including Days of Our Lives, Shooter, Lucifer, Teen Wolf, and Tell Me a Story.

Her role as Courtney Sax in an episode of Netflix’s Lucifer was one of her well-received performances.

Besides her career, the American actress is also a committed partner and a mother of a three and half-year-old son.

Her Relationship with Entrepreneur Donae Burston

Terrell has been in a long time relationship with Donae Burston since before September 2016.

While the couple hasn't confirmed if they are married, their social media accounts boast numerous posts that show their relationship timeline and closeness as partners.

A casual scroll of their Instagram profile also shows that Terrell and Burston made their social media debut on September 3, 2016 — 11 months before giving birth to their first son Noah.

"Excuse me miss, what's your name?" Burston captioned their first picture on Instagram.

After that post, the couple has been regularly appearing on one another's feed. On occasions, they have called each other parents, family, and love.

In her latest Instagram post, uploaded on May 25, 2021, Terrell enjoyed a glass of wine with her partner and complimented the summer vibes.

Welcomed a Son Together

Terrell and her potential husband gave birth to their first child on August 12, 2017. The Tell Me a Story actress had taken to Instagram on August 17 to confirm the news.

“Thank you my love @thurstonburstoniii for this beautiful gift,” she captioned. “You [son] arrived on 8-12-17...a day that forever changed my life in the most beautiful way. My hope and prayer is that you, Noah, my beautiful baby boy will be a light of love in this world because you sure are in mine.”

She had announced her pregnancy the same year on April 17 through Instagram.

Presently, the family of three lives together in Miami, United States.

Burston Owns a Wine Company

Terrell’s partner Burston is an entrepreneur who owns a rosé wine brand La Fête du Rosé, which was founded in 2019.

But he has been involved in the wine and spirits industry for over 15 years and worked with Dom Perignon, Moet & Chandon, and Hennessy.

The winemaker started his business with a mission to have people of color as his target audience.

Today, he has successfully imported the first Black-owned rosé label from France's Saint-Tropez to the United States.

"I want to be the No. 1 luxury rosé wine brand in the world, full stop," Burston told Wine Spectator in May 2021. "I think for me what was more important was the growth and to ensure that I had the resources needed to grow the brand with someone that could bring the tools I needed."

Burston, who has over 6 thousand followers on Instagram, also promotes the brand with his partner. They are often snapped in the brand's promotional pictures and campaigns.
