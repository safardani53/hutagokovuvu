Inside Gwyneth Paltrow, Dakota Johnson, Chris Martins Dynamic

October 06, 2024
blog

Gwyneth Paltrow and Chris Martin remained friends after their divorce, and even his girlfriend, Dakota Johnson, has been welcomed into the family.

Paltrow and Martin tied the knot in December 2003, later welcoming daughter Apple and son Moses in May 2004 and April 2006, respectively. When the pair announced their split in March 2014, they noted that they still loved each other “very much.”

Paltrow subsequently filed for divorce in April 2015, and the proceedings were finalized in July 2016. She moved on with Brad Falchuk, while Martin sparked a romance with Johnson.

“Gwyneth loves Dakota and vice versa. She really likes her and who she is as a person,” a source told Us in October 2019, noting Paltrow’s stance that anyone who enriched Martin’s life would do the same for her children. “She cares deeply for him and wants him to be happy.”
