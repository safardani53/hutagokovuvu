Rashaad Coward is an American football player who spent five seasons in the NFL and currently plays for the XFL team, Houston Roughnecks (2024- present).

After three seasons in the Bears under two head coaches John Fox & Matt Nagy, he toured with other NFL teams: the Pittsburgh Steelers, Jacksonville Jaguars, and Atlanta Falcons.

However, with no significant exposures, he signed with the Houston Roughnecks of the XFL on December 11, 2023.

In 2021, she accidentally leaked the news of her husband signing with the Steelers during her streaming of Call of Duty.
