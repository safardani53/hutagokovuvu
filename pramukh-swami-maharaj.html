Who is Pramukh Swami Maharaj:

Pramukh Swami Maharaj is a famous Religious Leader. He was born on December 7, 1921 and his birthplace is India. Pramukh is also well known as, Hindu guru and leader in the international Bochasanwasi Shri Akshar Purushottam Swaminarayan Sanstha (BAPS) organization. Under his leadership, the religious group grew to include more than a million members in numerous countries.

Pramukh is originated from India. He and Swami Vivekananda were both important Indian religious leaders.
