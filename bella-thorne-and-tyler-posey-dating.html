It’s only been one month since we learned that Bella Thorne and Gregg Sulkin have ended their relationship after just over a year of dating.

It’s not hard to see how they were misled.

The video in question showed Posey standing next to a sign that read, “Gay St.” and telling the camera:

“This is me. I am this and this is me. I’ve never felt more alive. I’m gay!”

“I am a big proponent of love over hate, and standing together during divisive times,” Posey later clarified on Twitter.

“[Part 2]: Although I’m not gay, I fully support the LGBTQ community. This was a moment intended to reflect that.”
