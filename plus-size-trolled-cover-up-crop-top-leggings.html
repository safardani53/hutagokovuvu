NO matter how confident you are, most of us will have a few items we avoid in our wardrobe.

Despite trolls regularly attacking her on social media though,  plus-size influencer Amber Baldwyn refuses to give up her crop tops and wears see-through leggings with confidence.

Whilst she might not be a household name just yet, this Tiktok star has amassed a huge following on the app thanks to her body-positive content.

Rather than hiding her curves, Amber regularly shows how to style them with outfits from brands like Shein, PrettyLittleThing and other affordable brands.

And more risqué clothes don’t intimidate her, with Amber styling a white crop top with semi-see-through leggings in a cheeky late-night photoshoot.

Despite her confident appearance, the young content creator has had her battles too with her struggling to find gym wear that makes her feel comfortable.
